Andrew Maurer

I have spent the last 5 years of my life serving differently abled individuals, as well as providing in person care for amputees. This work requires a Masters of Science in Orthotics and Prosthetics. I acquired that diploma in 2018, at Loma Linda University in Loma Linda, California. I have always sought to be an individual that enables others. Learning new skills, and expanding my knowledge base is crucial in order to be the most effective instrument for positive change in our world. I began the journey toward web development in late 2021, to facilitate that desire. Following 6 months of bootcamps focused on full stack development, I recognized a passion for creating functional, creative websites and web applications. Through the combination of my healthcare experience and learned web development experience, I have created one large scale website for use by all professionals in my field, as well as a potential source of information for patients. This website with the domain name- pxpartspicker.com, has proven of great help in a field where education is less a way of life, and more a hindrance to being productive. Proper resources are key for the drive toward a more efficient healthcare system. Even though my work is a drop in the bucket, it only takes a few more people to fill it to the brim.
